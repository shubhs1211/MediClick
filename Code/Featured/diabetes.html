• People can often have diabetes and be completely unaware. The main reason for this is that the symptoms, when seen on their own, seem harmless. However, the earlier diabetes is diagnosed the greater the chances are that serious complications, which can result from having diabetes, can be avoided.

  • Common symptoms of diabetes The most common signs and symptoms of diabetes are:
    1.Frequent urination
    2.Disproportionate thirst
    3.Intense hunger
    4.Weight gain
    5.Unusual weight loss
    6.Increased fatigue
    7.Irritability
    8.Blurred vision
    9.Cuts and bruises don't heal properly or quickly
    10.More skin and/or yeast infections
    11.Itchy skin
    12.Gums are red and/or swollen
    13.Frequent gum disease/infection
    14.Sexual dysfunction (men)
    15.Numbness or tingling, especially in your feet and hands
    We will examine each of these symptoms in more detail.
