Dr. Rajneesh Jain is a top experienced and renowned Cardiologist in Delhi and the National Capital Region (NCR). He is recognised for groundbreaking work in adult cardiac catheterisation, angiography, and handling acute coronary emergencies including primary angioplasties in patients of acute myocardial infarction with and without cardiogenic shock. He is also experienced in percutaneous non-coronary interventions such as peripheral angiographies and peripheral angioplasties (including renal artery stenting and carotid artery stenting), percutaneous balloon valvuloplasties of all valves, percutaneous IVC filter insertions for pulmonary embolisms, permanent pacemaker implantation (single and dual chambers).

Since 1986, Dr. Jain has been helping cardiac patients to maintain a healthy heart and live a healthy life. In the 28 years of his career he has performed around 15,000 coronary angiographies and around 3,000 angioplasties. Presently he is working as Senior Interventional Cardiologist and Co-Director Cath Lab at Dharma Vira Heart Centre, Sir Ganga Ram Hospital New Delhi. The mission of the centre is to provide quality heart care at affordable prices to our patients.

Dr. Ranjeesh Jain is one of the few interventional cardiologists in Delhi & NCR who performs a majority of procedures such as angiographies, angioplasties & stenting through the radial route (from the arm) which is minimally invasive and hence advantageous for the patient. He is expert in "drop in and walk out on the same day" for a majority of procedures.

Dr. Jain's areas of specialisation include:

  • ECG opinion
  • Arrhythmias
  • Cardiovascular diseases
  • Coronary angioplasty & stenting
  • Radio frequency ablation
  • ASD and PDA device closure
  • Treatment of cardiac valve diseases (balloon mitral valvotomy, etc.)
  • Coronary angiography
  • Rotablation
  • Pacemakers
  • AICD
  • Cardiac resynchronisation therapy (CRT-P and CRT-D)
  • Heart failure
  • Hypertension
  • Echocardiography including TMT & stress echo
  • High cholesterol (Dyslipidemia).

 

Dr. Jain holds an MBBS and MD from Government Medical College in Amritsar. He also obtained the DM superspeciality degree through GB Pant Hospital, New Delhi, in 1995. After completing the DM course, he spent two and a half years as Senior Research Associate at GB Pant Hospital. He then moved to the Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education and Research in Chandigarh, where he was involved in teaching as an Assistant Professor of Cardiology. He returned to Delhi as Consultant Interventional Cardiologist at Sir Ganga Ram Hospital in January 1999, moving up the career path to his current status.

An award-winning student whilst pursuing his MBBS, Dr. Rajneesh Jain has numerous publications to his name in peer-reviewed journals. He has presented some of his papers at professional conferences and meets in India and internationally, and has regularly attended such events as a delegate or invited faculty over the years. Dr. Jain was the primary operator of four cases of complex coronary intervention out of 60 best cases in the world presented at PCR Paris in May 2004 as part of the European Paris Course in revascularisation, and primary operator of two cases of complex peripheral interventions presented at the Transcatheter Therapeutics event at Washington in October 2004.
